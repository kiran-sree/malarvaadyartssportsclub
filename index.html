About

Welcome to our page, where we share the heartwarming story of our club and the positive impact we're making in the beautiful town of Karthikappally, Alappuzha, Kerala, India. Since its inception on February 5, 2014, our club has been dedicated to bringing smiles to the faces of the people we are linked with, while also actively contributing to the betterment of our community. Join us as we explore the incredible journey of our club and the meaningful work we do. We strongly believe in the concept of giving back to our community. Through collaborative efforts and partnerships, we actively engage in initiatives that address the specific needs of Karthikappally. From educational programs and vocational training to healthcare campaigns and environmental conservation, we strive to empower and uplift our community in every possible way. Our club, founded in 2014, stands as a beacon of hope and positivity in Karthikappally, Alappuzha, Kerala, India. Through our unwavering dedication to spreading smiles and empowering our community, we strive to make a meaningful difference in the lives of those we touch. Join us in our mission as we continue to build a better tomorrow, one smile at a time.
